Research Question - How many schools in California are within 10 miles of a recorded wildfire site?

Methods

To create this map I used the buffer and intersect methods. Once I had found a polygon of the recorded wildfires in California i had to create a 10 mile buffer aroud it. I did this by creating a new .geojson of the polygon in the 3857 projection and then using the buffer tool on that new polygon. Once I had my new buffer polygon I added a vector of the schools in California and also put tnat is 3857. I then used the intersect tool to get just the schools that fell within the wildfire buffer.
